History

The first electric guitar patent was made in 1937.Invented by G.D. Beauchamp, the instrument was known as the Rickenbacker Frying Pan.

Gibson made its first production guitar in 1937 which had some popularity but suffered from unequal loudness across the six strings. Gibson will eventually become a major electric guitar production company, making well-known models like the Les Paul and SG.

During the 1950s the electric guitar industry had explosive growth which lead to many different types of electric guitars to be made like the Stratocaster and Les Paul.
