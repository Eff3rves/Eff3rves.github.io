WHAT IS VECTOR-SHOT
A fast pace movement shooter in first person. It is a 1v1 intense match where the player has to eliminate the opponent 3 times to win.
Players can choose between 3 different guns, rifle, shotgun and sniper to use in battle. Each gun also does a higher damage after using the powerup.
To power up the weapons, the player will have to move around the arena to charge up a "Speedometer". Players can choose to activate the powerup and enter "Overrdrive" which cause the weapons to do more damage.
Players can also choose one movement ability, either teleport or grapple to help charge up the bar speedometer faster. Each ability has a differnt max bar for speedometer making the player's approach different when using different abilities and encouraging players to try out different combinations of ability and weapon.
