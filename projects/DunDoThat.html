DEMON CHEF MAYHEM
 This is one of my recent projects where I worked with a team of both designers and developers.

 Demon Chef Mayhem is a game about a chef in kitchen hell (literally), using food ingredients as projectiles to slay enemies and collect their drop to make more food.

 The game is built in Unreal Engine 5. I am the lead developer of this project where I had to do a lot of team management and coordination of the design and development team. In addition, I was also responsible for making the toon shader, the UIs, integrating assests from the designer and the merging mechanic of the food.
