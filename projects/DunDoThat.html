WHAT IS DON'T DO THAT
A meta game similar to The Stanley Parable where the player is a playtester and has the job of playinng throught thhe levels. If the player does not listen to the narator or act out of pocket, secrets may be discovered as they eventualy break the level.
The game is part of the submissions on Brackey's Game Jam 2025 where it was placed 113 out of 2000 submissions with a postive general feedback.
