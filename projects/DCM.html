WHAT IS DEMON CHEF MAYHEM
Demon Chef Mayhem is about a young chef working in hell's kitchen.
The player can use their spoon to knock enemies back which will make the enemies drop ingredients thats part of their body, using the ingredients picked up, the player can combine the ingredients to make projectiles that can be used to do more damage to the monsters.
The whole game is set in 1 continuous level with 1 final boss at the end.
